Privacy Policy for Bree English Learning App Introduction Welcome to Bree, an application designed to help users learn English. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data handling when you use our application. No Data Collection Policy We are proud to inform our users that Bree: Does not collect any personal information Does not require user registration Does not store or transmit any user data to external servers Only saves your learning progress locally on your device Local Storage All of your progress and settings are stored exclusively in your device's local storage. This means: Your learning data remains on your device Your data is not accessible to us or any third parties Your data will be deleted if you uninstall the application Third-Party Services Our application does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect user data. Device Permissions Bree may request certain device permissions to function properly, such as: Storage access (to save your progress locally) Notification permissions (if you enable learning reminders) These permissions are used solely for app functionality and not for data collection. Children's Privacy Our application is suitable for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no specific privacy concerns regarding children's use of our app. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
